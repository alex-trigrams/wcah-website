About us

We're Here to Help You Thrive

At West Coast Allied Health, we are passionate about helping our community live healthier, stronger, and more active lives. Based in Yanchep, we proudly support individuals and families throughout the surrounding areas with personalised, evidence-based allied health services tailored to their unique needs.

Whether you're recovering from an injury, managing a chronic health condition, improving your mobility, or working towards your health and fitness goals, our team is here to guide and support you every step of the way. We take the time to understand your goals and create individualised treatment plans that focus on achieving long-term results, not just short-term relief.

Our mission is simple: to empower every person with the knowledge, support, and confidence to move better, feel stronger, and enjoy a healthier quality of life.

How we help

Where we can help

01

Injury Rehabilitation

Recover with confidence through personalised exercise programs designed to reduce pain, restore movement, and help you safely return to work, sport, and everyday life. We manage all types of injuries, including recent or long-term issues, and help you understand how a focused exercise plan can improve your confidence, pain, and independence.

02

Chronic Condition Management

Evidence-based exercise programs tailored to help manage chronic conditions, improve your health, and enhance your quality of life through safe and sustainable movement. We work closely with you to build long-term habits that support better health and ongoing independence.

03

Strength & Conditioning

Build strength, improve performance, and reduce injury risk with individualised training programs designed to help you achieve your health, fitness, or sporting goals. Whether you're new to exercise or an experienced athlete, we'll create a program that challenges and supports you every step of the way.

04

Group Classes

Enjoy supportive, supervised exercise sessions in a welcoming group environment, designed to improve strength, mobility, balance, and overall wellbeing for all fitness levels. Our classes are led by qualified Exercise Physiologists, ensuring every exercise is safe, effective, and tailored to your abilities.

Meet Our Valued Team Members

Our accredited EPs bring the expertise. The reason people keep coming back is that they genuinely listen and build programs that actually fit your life.

photo: Matthew Wood
Matthew Wood
Accredited Exercise Physiologist

Matt graduated as an Accredited Exercise Physiologist in 2018.

He has treated a diverse range of conditions which include Musculoskeletal, Cardiovascular, Neurological, and Mental Health, along with many more. He specialises in musculoskeletal injuries, chronic pain management, and strength and conditioning.

Matt's interest in the field originated from having a number of injuries throughout his life that caused many setbacks, and he understands the impact it can have on his client's physical and mental health.

He is an avid sports fan following both AFL and Cricket, and competes in ultra running events.
