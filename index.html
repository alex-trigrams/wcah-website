About us

Exercise physiologists who actually listen

We help people all over Perth move better and feel stronger. Some of our clients are coming back from an injury. Some are managing something like diabetes or arthritis. And some just want to keep doing the things they love, for longer.

Whatever brings you in, we build the program around you: your goals, your body, the week you actually have. It's one-on-one, it's grounded in real research, and it fits into your life rather than taking it over.

How we help

Where we can help

Four things we see a lot of. If yours isn't here, ask anyway. There's a good chance we can help.

01

Injury rehab

Coming back from an injury or surgery? We rebuild your strength and movement step by step, so you can get back to normal life without flaring things up again.

02

Chronic conditions

Exercise is one of the best tools for managing diabetes, heart disease and arthritis. We show you how to use it safely, and stick with you while it becomes a habit.

03

Strength & healthy ageing

Build strength, stay steady on your feet, and hold onto your independence as you get older. Small, regular work that pays off for years.

04

Mental health

Movement helps your head as much as your body. We use it to lift your mood, sleep better, and put a bit of routine back in the week.
